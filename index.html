Welcome

Hi! I’m Prerana Kulla, a freshman at UNC-Chapel Hill from Harrisburg, NC. Before coming to Carolina, I graduated from the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics at Morganton. Currently, I am pursuing a double major in Psychology and Computer Science with a minor in Business of Health.

On campus, I serve as a Residential Computing Consultant (RCC) with ResNET. I am also an active member of Carolina Women in Business, CPALS, and Bharat Sangeet. I work as a Research Assistant at the Schiller Lab, where I study the correlation between estradiol and neural and molecular responses to reward in women.

Looking ahead, I hope to gain more experience in psychiatric research specifically focused on women's health.
