Research Statement

My research agenda focuses on exploring the neurobiological mechanisms underlying psychiatric and affective disorders in adolescent girls, specifically examining how the pubertal surge in estradiol influences neural reward circuitry and emotional reactivity. My future research goals aim to bridge the gap in understanding the temporal link between pubertal development and mesolimbic dopamine pathway remodeling by utilizing longitudinal functional neuroimaging and hormonal profiling.

Biodegradability of Cellulose in Filter Paper

January 2024 - April 2024

Focus: Environmental Science, Mycology, Waste Management

Evaluated how various ink formulations impact fungal cellulose breakdown. Findings showed water/algae-based inks were biodegradable, while oil/alcohol-based inks inhibited the process.
